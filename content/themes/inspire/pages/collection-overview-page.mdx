The collection overview page (accessible by default via `/collections` on your webshop) shows a visual overview of all available collections in your store. This is a handy starting point for visitors to quickly navigate through your different product categories.

The display of the individual collection cards is managed via the general theme settings (under 'Collection & Brand Cards'). On this page, you specifically manage the layout of the overview itself.

### General Layout & Design

- **Color scheme:** Choose the default color scheme (background and text color) for the entire overview.
- **Pagination Color Scheme:** You can make the pagination elements (the dots or numbers to browse to the next page) stand out more by giving them their own distinct color scheme.
- **Remove bottom margin (No margin bottom):** Check this to remove the whitespace at the very bottom of the section. This is ideal if you want the footer or a subsequent section to sit tightly against the overview.
- **Custom Class:** A setting for advanced users who want to attach extra, custom CSS code to this specific page.

### Display & Pagination

- **Collections per page (Per page):** Determine the maximum number of collections loaded on a single page before pagination appears. You can set this with the slider from 1 up to a maximum of 50 collections (the default is 24).
