With the **Video section**, you can easily add moving imagery to your webshop. Whether it is an atmospheric background video, a product demonstration, or a brand story: video grabs attention immediately. You can choose to upload a video directly to Shopify or use a link from platforms such as YouTube or Vimeo.

### General Layout & Whitespace

- **Color scheme:** Choose the desired color scheme for the texts and the background of the section.
- **Section display (Type):**
  - **Full width (Edge-to-edge):** The video fills the full width of the screen.
  - **Container (Box):** The video is placed within a neat frame.
- **Spacing:** Manage the margins and padding for the top and bottom to give the video exactly the right amount of space on the page.

### Video Settings

You have two options for the source of your video:

- **Shopify Hosted (Uploaded):** Upload your own video file (e.g., .mp4) directly to Shopify.
  - **Autoplay:** Have the video play automatically (sound is muted by default).
  - **Loop:** Ensure the video repeats continuously.
- **Embed (YouTube/Vimeo):** Paste a link from an external video website. 
- **Aspect ratio:** Determine the shape of the video player (e.g., the classic **16:9**, the wide **21:9**, or a square **1:1**).

### Cover Image (Thumbnail)

To prevent an empty black area from being visible before the video loads, you can set a **Cover image**:
- **Image:** Upload a still frame from the video or a specially designed thumbnail.
- **Alt text:** Add a description for search engines and accessibility.

### Text & Alignment

You can accompany the video with a title and a short description:

- **Title & Formatting:** Add a heading and determine the SEO HTML tag, the visual size, and the font weight.
- **Content (Rich text):** Use this field for an additional explanation below the title.
- **Alignment:** Choose whether to display the texts to the left, centered, or to the right of (or below) the video.
