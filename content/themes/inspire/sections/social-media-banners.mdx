  Je hebt de volledige controle over hoe de logo-banners worden getoond via het
  **Weergave type**:


  - **Grid (Raster):** De logo's staan in een vast stramien. Bepaal exact
  hoeveel kolommen (logo's naast elkaar) je wilt tonen voor desktop (2 t/m 6),

  de logo-vlakken, van vierkant (1:1) tot diverse liggende of staande formaten.

  Introduceer je partners of merken met een tekstblok boven de logo's:

  - **Actieknoppen (Buttons):** Voeg maximaal twee knoppen toe onder de tekst.

    - Vink **Secundaire knopstijl** aan voor een transparante 'outline' look.
    - Voeg optioneel een icoontje toe (zoals een pijltje naar rechts).

  alternatieve tekst voor SEO).

  - **Link:** Maak het logo klikbaar door er een URL aan te koppelen.

With the **Social Media Banners** section, you can stylishly display your social posts and banners, logos of brands you carry, partners you work with, or social media channels where you are active. This is a powerful way to project authority and trust to your visitors.

### General Layout & Background

Set up the base of the section to ensure it aligns perfectly with the rest of your page:

- **Color scheme:** Choose the color scheme for the background and text of this section.
- **Section display (Type):** Determine whether the background spans the full width (**Full width / Edge-to-edge**) or is centered within a frame (**Box / Container**).
- **Background image:** You can give the section extra character by setting a background image (with the option for a separate photo for mobile).
- **Height & Background height:** Choose whether the section automatically grows with the logos (**Auto**) or work with a fixed height (**Fixed**). With a fixed height, use the slider (0-100%) to determine how far the background color extends.

### Display: Slider or Grid

You have full control over how the logo banners are displayed via the **Display type**:

- **Grid:** The logos are in a fixed layout. Determine exactly how many columns (logos next to each other) you want to show for desktop (2 to 6), tablet (1 to 4), and mobile (1 to 3).
- **Slider:** The logos are in a swipeable carousel. Set how many logos are visible at once. Use 'half' numbers (such as 1.5 or 2.5) on mobile to indicate that visitors can swipe.
  - **Navigation & Effects:** Enable pagination dots, let the logos stagger playfully (**Alternate image offset**), or let the slider visually extend outside the frame (**Carousel outside container**).

**Image ratio:** For both types, determine the shape of the logo areas, from square (1:1) to various landscape or portrait formats.

### Text & Buttons

Introduce your partners or brands with a text block above the logos:

- **Alignment:** Align the title and introductory text to the left, center, or right.
- **Title Formatting:** Fully customize the SEO HTML tag, the visual size (H1 to display), and the font weight of the title.
- **Content (Rich text):** Use this field for a short explanation or a call-to-action.
- **Action buttons (Buttons):** Add up to two buttons below the text.
  - Give the button a label and link the appropriate URL.
  - Check **Secondary button style** for a transparent 'outline' look.
  - Optionally add an icon (such as a right arrow).

### Adding blocks (Banners)

You build the content of this section with individual **Banner** blocks. Add a block for each logo:

- **Logo (Image):** Upload the image of the logo here. *Tip: Use transparent .PNG or .SVG files for the cleanest result.*
- **Title:** The name of the brand or channel (also used as alt-text for SEO).
- **Link:** Make the logo clickable by linking a URL to it.
