The **Customer Reviews** section is essential for building trust with your visitors. By showcasing positive experiences from existing customers, you lower the barrier for new customers to make a purchase. This section is presented in a dynamic slider, allowing you to display many reviews without making the page too long.

### General Layout & Design

- **Color scheme:** Choose the color scheme for the background and text of the section.
- **Section display (Type):** Determine whether the background of the section spans the full width (**Full width / Edge-to-edge**) or is centered within a frame (**Box / Container**).
- **Spacing (Whitespace):** Manage the margin and padding for the top and bottom to give the section the right amount of space on the page. Choose between default spacing or no space (None).

### Slider Settings

The reviews are displayed in a carousel. You can fine-tune the display per device:

- **Reviews in view (Slides per view):**
  - **Desktop:** Choose from 3 to 8 reviews simultaneously, or select 'auto'.
  - **Tablet & Mobile:** Choose whole or 'half' views here (such as 1.5 or 2.5). A half slide at the edge of the screen shows visitors that there is more to read.
- **Navigation & Effects:**
  - **Pagination:** Show navigation dots below the slider.
  - **Playful staggering (Alternate offset):** Check this to place the review cards alternately slightly higher or lower for a dynamic effect.
  - **Carousel overflow:** Determine whether the slider is cut off cleanly at the frame or visually extends to the edge of the screen.

### Text & Buttons (Introduction)

You can place an introductory text above the reviews:

- **Alignment:** Align the title and text to the left, center, or right.
- **Title Formatting:** Add a catchy heading (e.g., "What our customers are saying") and adjust the SEO HTML tag, visual size (H1 to display), and font weight.
- **Content (Rich text):** Use this field for a brief explanation or a thank you to your customers.
- **Action buttons (Buttons):** Add up to two buttons (e.g., "Leave a review"). Give the button a label, link, icon, and choose between a normal or 'outline' style.

### Adding blocks (Reviews)

You can fill this section with two types of blocks:

#### 1. Review (Manual review)

Use this block to manually add reviews you have received via email or social media, for example.

- **Rating:** Select the number of stars (1 to 5) to be displayed.
- **Title:** A short summary or the name of the customer.
- **Content:** The actual text of the review.

#### 2. InStijl Review Block (Automatic reviews)

If you use a supported review app, you can add this block to automatically load reviews into the slider.

- **Max length:** Determine after how many characters the text should be automatically shortened to keep the cards in the slider at a uniform height.
