The **Newsletter section** is an essential tool for building a customer list. It offers visitors a simple way to leave their email address so you can keep them informed about new products, promotions, or inspiration.

When a visitor signs up, their email address is automatically added to your customer list in Shopify with the tag 'Newsletter'.

### General Layout & Design

Customize the section so it either stands out or blends subtly into your page:

- **Color scheme:** Choose the desired color scheme for the background and texts.
- **Section display (Type):** - **Full width (Edge-to-edge):** The background color spans the full width of the screen.
  - **Container (Box):** The section is placed within a neat frame.
- **Spacing (Whitespace):** Manage the margins and padding for the top and bottom to create the right breathing room around the section.
- **Image:** You can add an image that appears next to or near the sign-up form to make the section visually more appealing.

### Content & Text

Convince visitors to sign up with a strong message:

- **Alignment:** Choose whether the text and the input field should be aligned to the left, center, or right.
- **Title & Formatting:** Write an inviting heading (e.g., "Sign up for our newsletter"). You can fully customize the SEO HTML tag, the visual size, and the font weight of the title.
- **Text (Rich text):** Use the text editor for a brief explanation of what the customer can expect (e.g., "Receive 10% discount on your first order").
- **Privacy note:** Add a small line of text below the form (e.g., "You can unsubscribe at any time") to gain the visitor's trust.

> **Tip:** The texts for the email input field and the submit button are automatically pulled from your theme's language settings, ensuring they are always consistent with the rest of your shop.
