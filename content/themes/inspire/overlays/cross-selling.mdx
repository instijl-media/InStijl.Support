The **Cross-selling popup** is an essential tool for increasing your Average Order Value (*AOV*). This window appears immediately after a customer adds a product to their cart. It not only confirms the action but also encourages the customer to view relevant, matching products.

### General Settings

- **Color scheme:** Determine the colors of the modal. Preferably use a scheme that projects a clean look, but ensures the action buttons stand out effectively.
- **Custom Class:** For specific CSS adjustments by developers.

### Confirmation & Product Details

At the top of the modal, the customer sees a clear confirmation:

- **Status:** An "Added to cart" notification with a checkmark.
- **Product Overview:** The image, title, and price of the item just added are displayed.
- **Direct Link:** A button to go directly to the cart page to complete the purchase.

### Recommendations (Slider)

Below the confirmation, the section automatically displays relevant products (based on Shopify's recommendation algorithm). You can fully fine-tune the display of these products:

- **Title & Formatting:** Set an inviting heading (e.g., "Complete your order"). You can adjust the visual size and font weight of this title.
- **Slides per view:** Determine how many recommendations are visible at once on Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile.
  - *Tip: On mobile, use the '1.5' setting to indicate that the customer can swipe right for more suggestions.*
- **Slider Navigation:** Enable pagination dots for better navigation through the recommendations.
- **Playful Staggering (Alternate offset):** Check this to place the product cards alternately slightly higher or lower for a dynamic effect within the modal.

> **Note**: In the editor's 'Design Mode', sample products are shown so you can evaluate the layout immediately. In the live shop, the actual relevant products will be loaded here.
