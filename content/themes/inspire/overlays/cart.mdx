The **Cart Sidebar** is an essential part of your webshop's user-friendliness. Instead of sending the visitor directly to a new page, a panel slides into the screen from the right side. This keeps the customer in the 'shopping flow' while providing an immediate overview of their current selection.

### General Settings

The settings for this sidebar are focused on a clean and organized display:

- **Color scheme:** Determine the color scheme for the sidebar. You can choose a light variant for a fresh look, or a dark scheme to put the focus on the content.
- **Custom Class:** For advanced styling by developers, such as adjusting the width or shadow of the sidebar.

### Content & Functionality

The sidebar is intelligently structured and shows all relevant information about the selected products:

- **Product details:** For each item in the cart, the image, title, and quantity are displayed.
- **Pricing:** Both the total price per line and any unit prices are directly visible.
- **Subscriptions & Properties:** If a product is part of a subscription (selling plan) or if custom properties (such as engraving or an upload) have been added, these are neatly listed under the product title.
- **Total overview:** The current total amount of the order is always displayed at the bottom of the sidebar.

### Actions

To stimulate conversion, the sidebar offers direct navigation options:

- **View cart:** A button that leads the customer to the full shopping cart page for more details.
- **Checkout:** The primary action button that sends the customer directly to the secure payment environment.
- **Additional payment methods:** If activated in Shopify, accelerated checkout options (such as Apple Pay, Google Pay, or PayPal) are also shown here for even faster processing.

> **Tip:** Ensure the color scheme of the cart sidebar aligns well with your header, as this is where customers often start the interaction with their basket.
