This manual will help you get started with the **Inspire** theme.

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, we assume that you have:

- A Shopify webshop
- Basic knowledge of the Shopify Admin interface

### 1. Installing the theme

**Inspire** is a *premium* Shopify theme and can only be purchased via the Shopify Theme Store. Visit our theme in the [theme store](http://themes.shopify.com/themes/inspire/) and click on '**Try theme**'. A demo of the theme will be added to your webshop immediately. Installation may take a few seconds to a few minutes.

### 2. Customizing the theme

Now that the demo of the theme is installed, you can start working with the theme settings right away. Follow these steps:

1. [Theme settings](http://instijl.support/themes/inspire/theme-settings) - First, set the basic settings and brand identity correctly.
2. [Header](https://www.instijl.support/themes/inspire/header) & [footer](https://www.instijl.support/themes/inspire/footer) - Customize the header and footer to match your brand.
3. Homepage settings - Build your own converting homepage.
4. [Collection](https://www.instijl.support/themes/inspire/collectionpage) & [Product page](https://www.instijl.support/themes/inspire/productpage) - Build your collection and product pages to your liking.
5. [Other pages](https://www.instijl.support/themes/inspire/other-pages) - Fill the remaining pages with the wide range of [theme sections](https://www.instijl.support/themes/inspire/sections).

### 3. Purchasing the theme

After installing the theme, you will automatically receive a demo version. Before you go live, it is necessary to complete the purchase of the theme:

1. In the Shopify Admin, go to **Online Store > Themes**
2. Click the 'Buy' button under 'Inspire'
3. Follow the steps to complete the purchase.
