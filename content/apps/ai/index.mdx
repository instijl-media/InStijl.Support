# InStijl AI

Welcome to the documentation for **InStijl AI**, an AI-powered product enrichment app for Lightspeed eCom.

## What it does

- Generates rich product descriptions
- Extracts and assigns specs and attributes
- Suggests new attribute concepts when missing in your shop
