With InStijl Postcode Check, orders will always reach the right person by validating addresses on each new order. Increase conversion and save costs on returns with automatic address additions in the Shopify Plus checkout.

Every shop owner has experienced this: the customer places an order but enters an incorrect or non-existent address. This is annoying for both parties. It sometimes takes a lot of time to fix the error and the customer relationship is damaged. And the return also costs money. To prevent this, InStijl Postcode Check automatically validates all adresses. When possible, the app even fixes wrong addresses automatically.
