  ## About review-sources


  Wanneer de app geïnstalleerd is in jouw Shopify webshop, kun je verbinding
  maken met een of meerdere review-systemen. Door een koppeling te maken met een
  of meerdere review 'bronnen' kunnen we je totale score berekenen en de widgets
  op basis van deze reviews genereren. Het koppelen verschild per
  review-systeem. Bekijk dus per systeem hoe je kunt koppelen.


  ## Kiyoh / Klantenvertellen


  Volg de onderstaande stappen om Kiyoh of Klantenvertellen reviews toe te
  voegen aan de Shop Review Widget.


  1. Klik binnen de Shop Review Widget app op 'Nieuwe review-bron toevoegen'

  2. Selecteer "Kiyoh / Klantenvertellen"

  3. Klik op "Toevoegen"

  4. Ga naar het tandwieltje van de zojuist toegevoegde review-bron

  5. Vul de XML feed van Kiyoh of Klantenvertellen in. Deze kun je vinden in je
  Kiyoh/Klantenvertellen dashboard.

  6. Activeer de review-bron, door de schakelaar aan te vinken.

  7. Wacht 1-2 minuten, de reviews zullen nu zichtbaar zijn!

  3. Klik op "Toevoegen"

  4. Ga naar het tandwieltje van de zojuist toegevoegde review-bron

  5. Vul de openbare url van jouw Trustpilot pagina in. 

  6. Activeer de review-bron, door de schakelaar aan te vinken.

## About review-sources

When the app is installed in your Shopify webshop, you can connect to one or more review systems. By linking to one or more review 'sources' we can calculate your total score and generate the widgets based on these reviews. Linking differs per review system. So check per system how to connect.

## Kiyoh / Klantenvertellen

Follow the steps below to add Kiyoh or Klantenvertellen reviews to the Shop Review Widget.

1. Within the Shop Review Widget app, click on "Add new review source.
2. Select "Kiyoh / Klantenvertellen"
3. Click on add
4. Go to the gear icon of the review source you just added
5. Enter the XML feed from Kiyoh or Klantenvertellen. This can be found in your Kiyoh/Klantenvertellen dashboard.
6. Activate the review source, by checking the switch.
7. Wait 1-2 minutes, the reviews will now be visible!

## Trustpilot

Follow the steps below to add Trustpilot reviews to the Shop Review Widget.

1. Within the Shop Review Widget app, click on "Add new review source.
2. Select "Trustpilot"
3. Click on "Add"
4. Go to the gear icon of the review source you just added
5. Enter the public url of your Trustpilot page.
6. Activate the review source, by checking the switch.
7. Wait 1-2 minutes, the reviews will now be visible!

## Webwinkelkeur

Follow the steps below to add Webwinkelkeur reviews to the Shop Review Widget.

1. Within the Shop Review Widget app, click "Add new review source.
2. Select "Webwinkelkeur"
3. Click on "Add"
4. Go to the cog icon of the review source you just added
5. Enter your own Webwinkelkeur ID and Webwinkelkeur API key. These can be found in the Webwinkelkeur dashboard.
6. Activate the review source, by checking the switch.
7. Wait 1-2 minutes, the reviews will now be visible!

## Feedback Company

Follow the steps below to add Feedback Company reviews to the Shop Review Widget.

1. Within the Shop Review Widget app, click on "Add new review source.
2. Select "Trustpilot"
3. Click on "Add"
4. Go to the gear icon of the review source you just added
5. Enter the public url of your Feedback Company page. Make sure you've got at least one published review.
6. Activate the review source, by checking the switch.
7. Wait 1-2 minutes, the reviews will now be visible!
