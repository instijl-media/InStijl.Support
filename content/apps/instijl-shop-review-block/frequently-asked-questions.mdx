### **Why do I need to create an account to use this app?**

Through your account you can access all our apps and app settings. This is even possible if you have multiple shops. Through your account we save your app settings.

### **I have linked my review system, but I don't see anything yet. How is that possible?**

It can take 1-3 minutes before the reviews are visible. This is because we need to connect to your review system. Please be patient, your reviews will be visible soon!
