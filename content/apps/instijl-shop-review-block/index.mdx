Follow the steps below to install Shop Review Widget in your Shopify webshop.

- Search for "Shop Review Widget" in [the Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/instijl-shop-review-block?locale=nl)  
Install the app via the Shopify App Store

You will now enter the installation process. Follow all the steps and fill in the required information. During installation, you can login with an existing account on My InStyle Media, or you can easily create a new account.

After installing, you need to connect to your review system. Go to the next page to see how to do this!
