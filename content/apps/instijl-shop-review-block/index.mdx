With Shop Review Widget, external webshop reviews are automatically converted to a customized review widget. You can customize these widgets so that they integrate seamlessly with your webshop theme.

Link KiyOh, Klantenvertellen, Webwinkelkeur, Feedback Company, Trustpilot, Trusted Shops and more with the app and all reviews are automatically updated every day across the entire webshop.
