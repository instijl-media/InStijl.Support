[InStijl.AI](http://InStijl.AI) uses a fair credit system to track your usage. Not every AI is equally powerful, and not every AI model is equally costly. In addition, we want to give you maximum insight into your AI consumption.

Each time you use one of the AI tools, it will cost you at least 1 credit. Think of things like translating or rewriting texts. Generating images is slightly more resource-intensive and can therefore cost around 5 credits, depending on the image size.

## Limits

Your credits reset monthly. This means you receive the same amount of credits again every month. Your monthly budget does not roll over. Check your account to see how many credits you can use each month.

## Need extra credits?

Do you temporarily need more credits for a one-off task? You can easily purchase extra credits at an affordable rate. Unlike monthly credits, these purchased credits remain valid until you have used them up. You never have to worry about your extra credits expiring.

If you haven't used up your extra credits by the end of the month, you will first spend your included subscription credits in the new month before your extra credits are touched again.

## Tracking usage

On the **'Usage & Credits'** page, you will find an overview of your consumed credits. This clear breakdown shows how many credits you have already spent, which tools you used them for, and how many you have left. This way, you know exactly what happened over the past month.
