[InStijl.AI](http://InStijl.AI) offers multiple subscription tiers, ensuring there is always one that fits your needs.

## How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions can be canceled monthly. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Subscriptions are valid for at least 1 online store, but certain plans can be activated across multiple webshops—ideal if you manage more than one store.

You can find the full terms, options, and pricing at [www.instijl.ai/billing.](https://www.instijl.ai/billing)
