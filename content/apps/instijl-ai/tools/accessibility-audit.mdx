The **Accessibility Audit** tool helps you check your online store for accessibility barriers and bottlenecks regarding EAA compliance (European Accessibility Act). By generating targeted, actionable reports, you can improve the usability of your webshop for all visitors and ensure you meet important guidelines.

## How do you start an Accessibility Audit?

You can find this tool in the dashboard. You have the option to automate the process or run a targeted check yourself:

### 1. Manual Audit
Want to check the current status of your online store or a specific landing page right away?
* **Determine Store URL:** By default, your linked store domain is used for the check. Want to test a specific page? You can easily overwrite this URL here.
* **Start:** Click the **'Run new audit'** button to start the analysis immediately.

### 2. Automated Audits
For continuous monitoring of your accessibility, you can automate the audits. Under the **Automated Audits** option, choose your desired frequency:
* Disabled (default setting)
* Every week
* Every 2 weeks
* Every month

## History and Reports

On the right side of your screen, you will find the overview of your completed checks. 

* **New Users:** If you are using the tool for the first time, you will see *"No reports yet"*. 
* **Viewing Reports:** Once you have completed an audit, it is saved in your **History**. Click on a specific report in the list to view the detailed results, identified barriers, and concrete action items for your online store.
