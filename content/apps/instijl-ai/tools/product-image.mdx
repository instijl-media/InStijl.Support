With the **Product Image** optimization tool, you effortlessly generate optimized product images based on your current, existing photos. After your manual approval, these are immediately added to your products as new, improved images.

> **Please note:** Image generation is only available from **Growth** onwards.

## How does product image optimization work?

Open the tool via your dashboard under **Image Optimization > Product Image** (or use `Ctrl/Cmd + K` and search for "Product Image"). You will now see a complete overview of all products in your store.

## Optimizing an image in steps

1. **Find your product:** Browse through the list using the pagination at the bottom of the page or use the search bar at the top.
2. **Synchronize data:** Have you just added new products to your online store and they aren't in the list yet? Click **'Refresh products'** at the top to load the latest data.
3. **Start the optimization:** In the 'Action' column, click the text **'Optimize images'** next to the product you want to handle.
4. **Enter your own prompt.** Enter what you want to change in your image. For example:   
*"Turn this into a product image with a white background."* or: *"Place the object in a suitable background to show how the customer can use the product."* or: "*Change the color of this product from blue to red."*
5. **Generate and Approve:** The AI tool analyzes your existing product image and generates an optimized variant.
6. **Add to shop:** Review the new image. Once you are satisfied and approve the image, it will automatically be added to your product as a new image in the online store. The tool never makes changes without your explicit approval.

## What can I do with the Product Image tool?

This tool specializes in:

- Quickly and easily improving the visual presentation of your products using AI.
- Generating new, optimized image variants without needing an external photo editor.
- Adjusting image colors
- Generating banners in your own corporate identity
- Creating neat product photos from messy photos
- Placing your clothing on a model in various poses
- **And much more!**
