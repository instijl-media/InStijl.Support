  Klik rechts bovenin op "Gehele batch herschrijven" om de batch te starten. Je

  Wanneer je je prompt hebt opgegeven wordt jouw prompt eerst getest op het
  eerste categorie in de selectie. Klik op 'Test uitvoeren' om dit te

The **Category Writer** is an AI tool that specializes in writing copy for categories. Generate engaging, SEO-friendly category content with internal links that improve customer engagement. Clean up content. Build internal links using our context-aware AI. Or generate FAQ-structured data for your SEO.

## How does the Category Writer work?

Open the Category Writer via **SEO & Content > Category Writer.** You will now see an overview of all your categories. Search for and select a category, then click on '**Rewrite**'.

A screen will now open displaying the current category information. Additionally, you can enter a prompt to indicate exactly what you want to change. Try out one of the suggested 'Quick prompts' to see what's possible. 

Example prompts:

> Optimize the title and short summary (description field) for SEO, focusing on the most relevant keywords from the category details.

or: 

> Repair and clean up strange characters, faulty HTML tags, or broken formatting caused by a poor import.

You can find more examples at the bottom of the page.

Next, click on 'Generate content'. The AI tool will get to work. You will then receive an overview of all suggested changes. Next, select the fields you want to update. Finally, click on 'Approve and update' to save the changes immediately. It's also possible to cancel and rewrite the prompt. **The AI Tool does not make any changes to your shop until you explicitly give your approval.**

## Editing / rewriting categories in batches

In addition to editing categories one by one, it's also possible to run your own prompts in large batches. This is ideal if you want to have a large number of categories written or rewritten using AI.

> **Please note:** Batch edits are only available from the Growth subscription onwards.

### Selecting categories

You can select categories by using the search function. The Batch function only works for the categories in your current search query.

### Starting a batch

Click on "Rewrite entire batch" in the top right corner to start the batch. First, you will see an overview of the category selection. You can also write your prompt directly, or select one of the example prompts. Click on 'Proceed to testing' to test your prompt on 1 category first.

### Testing a batch

Once you have provided your prompt, it will first be tested on the first category in the selection. Click on 'Run test' to confirm this.

You will now see the result of your prompt. Review the result and select the fields you want to keep. Also look at the total estimated credits your prompt will cost. Then click on 'Approve and start task'. 

### Insufficient credits for my batch prompt

With large batch tasks, it might happen that your subscription does not have enough credits available. You can purchase a one-time credit top-up to carry out the task anyway. Of course, you can also reduce your category selection so you need fewer credits.

### Can I undo my changes?

Once you have approved changes, it is not possible to reverse them. However, you can use our built-in backup tool to restore past data.

## What can I do with the Category Writer?

The Category Writer specializes in:

- Writing new SEO copy based on full category information
- Building internal links to related products and categories
- Improving spelling and grammar
- Converting basic texts into your own Tone of Voice
- Generating meta titles, meta descriptions, and meta keywords
- Minor changes, such as removing specific brand names from content
- Adding an FAQ schema to category pages
- Rewriting text in a friendly / formal / informal / persuasive tone
- Building a better HTML structure
- Adding a bulleted list with USPs to category content
- Creating a short description based on content
- Incorporating specifications into category content
- Integrating keywords into existing category text
- **And much more!**
