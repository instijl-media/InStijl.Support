  producten wilt laten (her)schrijven met AI.


  > **Let op:** De batch-aanpassingen zijn alleen mogelijk vanaf het Growth
  abonnement

  Je kunt producten selecteren door de zoekfunctie te gebruiken. De
  Batch-functie werkt alleen voor de producten uit je huidige zoekopdracht.


  ### Batch starten


  Klik rechts bovenin op "Gehele batch herschrijven" om de batch te starten. Je
  krijgt eerst een overzicht van de product-selectie te zien. Je kunt ook direct

  Wanneer je je prompt hebt opgegeven wordt jouw prompt eerst getest op het
  eerste product in de selectie. Klik op 'Test uitvoeren' om dit te bevestigen.


  Je krijgt nu het resultaat van jouw prompt te zien. Beoordeel het resultaat en
  selecteer de velden die je wilt behouden. Kijk ook naar de totaal geschatte

  credits beschikbaar heeft. Je kunt eenmalig wat credits aanschaffen om de taak

  zodat je minder credits nodig hebt.

  backup-tool om data uit het verleden terug te zetten.

The **Product Writer** is an AI tool that specializes in writing copy for products. Generate engaging, SEO-friendly product content with internal links that improve customer engagement. Clean up content. Build internal links using our context-aware AI. Or generate FAQ-structured data for your SEO.

## How does the Product Writer work?

Open the Product Writer via **SEO & Content > Product Writer.** You will now see an overview of all your products. Search for and select a product, then click on '**Rewrite**'.

A screen will now open displaying the current product information. Additionally, you can enter a prompt to indicate exactly what you want to change. Try out one of the suggested 'Quick prompts' to see what's possible. 

Example prompts:

> Make the product description more appealing and persuasive, placing extra emphasis on the key features to increase conversion.

or: 

> Add a short FAQ section to the long HTML content and include valid schema.org FAQPage JSON-LD markup for those FAQs.

You can find more examples at the bottom of the page.

Next, click on 'Generate content'. The AI tool will get to work. You will then receive an overview of all suggested changes. Next, select the fields you want to update. Finally, click on 'Approve and update' to save the changes immediately. It's also possible to cancel and rewrite the prompt. **The AI Tool does not make any changes to your shop until you explicitly give your approval.**

## Editing / rewriting products in batches

In addition to editing products one by one, it's also possible to run your own prompts in large batches. This is ideal if you want to have a large number of products written or rewritten using AI.

> **Please note:** Batch edits are only available from the Growth subscription onwards.

### Selecting products

You can select products by using the search function. The Batch function only works for the products in your current search query.

### Starting a batch

Click on "Rewrite entire batch" in the top right corner to start the batch. First, you will see an overview of the product selection. You can also write your prompt directly, or select one of the example prompts. Click on 'Proceed to testing' to test your prompt on 1 product first.

### Testing a batch

Once you have provided your prompt, it will first be tested on the first product in the selection. Click on 'Run test' to confirm this.

You will now see the result of your prompt. Review the result and select the fields you want to keep. Also look at the total estimated credits your prompt will cost. Then click on 'Approve and start task'. 

### Insufficient credits for my batch prompt

With large batch tasks, it might happen that your subscription does not have enough credits available. You can purchase a one-time credit top-up to carry out the task anyway. Of course, you can also reduce your product selection so you need fewer credits.

### Can I undo my changes?

Once you have approved changes, it is not possible to reverse them. However, you can use our built-in backup tool to restore past data.

## What can I do with the Product Writer?

The Product Writer specializes in:

- Writing new SEO copy based on full product information
- Building internal links to related products and categories
- Improving spelling and grammar
- Converting supplier text into your own Tone of Voice
- Generating meta titles, meta descriptions, and meta keywords
- Minor changes, such as removing a brand name from the title
- Adding an FAQ schema to a product page
- Rewriting text in a friendly / formal / informal / persuasive tone
- Fixing strange characters caused by incorrect imports
- Adding a bulleted list with USPs to product content
- Creating a short description based on content
- Incorporating specifications into product content
- Integrating keywords into existing product text
- **And much more!**
