[InStijl.AI](http://InStijl.AI) is designed so that you can use all the different AI tools without needing any technical knowledge. Installing the app is a breeze!

## Lightspeed eCom c-series

1. Install the app via the App Store [link is coming soon]
2. You will be given the option to create an account or log in to an existing one. If you don't have an account yet, choose the 'Register account' option. Already have an account with InStijl.AI? Then log in with your existing account.
3. After installation, a [webshop profile](https://www.instijl.support/apps/instijl-ai/webhop-profile) will be created for you immediately.
4. The installation is now complete! Try out the app with your demo credits, or get a [subscription](https://www.instijl.support/apps/instijl-ai/usage-and-credits/subscriptions) to get started with all the AI tools right away!

Having trouble? Feel free to contact us: [www.instijlmedia.nl/contact/](https://www.instijlmedia.nl/contact/)

## Ecwid / Lightspeed eCom e-series
