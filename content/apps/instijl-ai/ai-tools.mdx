[InStijl.AI](http://InStijl.AI) consists of a collection of so-called "AI Tools". Each tool has its own specialty, such as writing copy, generating images, writing blog posts, building internal links, SEO optimization, etc.

On the left side, you can find an overview of all AI Tools. Each tool comes with a description of what you can do with it, but keep in mind: much more is possible thanks to the power of AI. 

Are you missing a specific tool or functionality? Please [contact us](https://www.instijl.support/nl/apps/instijl-ai/feedback-and-bugs), and who knows, this feature might be included in our next update!
