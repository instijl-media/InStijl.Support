You can find your webshop profile via the navigation menu on the left, under "Webshop Profile".

## What is a webshop profile?

Webshop profiles are a unique feature within InStijl.AI. A webshop profile includes a summary of your online store: what do you sell? What tone of voice do you use? What does your brand look like? What colors do you use? What are your most popular products and categories? Which keywords would you use to describe your webshop? 

All this information is bundled into your webshop profile.

## What is my webshop profile used for?

Your webshop profile is used as context for every AI tool you use. For example, the [Product Writer](https://www.instijl.support/apps/instijl-ai/tools/product-writer) knows exactly how you write your own texts. This is ideal for writing copy, generating images, creating blog posts, etc. Thanks to this context, the AI tools perform significantly better without you having to specify this in your prompts every time: the AI truly works for *your* webshop.

## Generating a webshop profile

When you install the app, a webshop profile is generated immediately. This happens completely automatically. We look at your webshop content, theme settings, color usage, and more. Would you like to regenerate your profile? Then click the 'Regenerate' button in the top right corner of the 'Webshop Profile' page.

## Editing a webshop profile

It is, of course, possible that you want to slightly adjust the generated webshop profile. Or perhaps you want to add some information. You can edit the webshop profile at any time by clicking the 'Edit profile' button in the top right corner of the 'Webshop Profile' page. You can now edit the various fields, such as Tone of voice, Branding, and Summary. Satisfied with your adjustments? Then click 'Save' to keep your changes. The next time you use one of the AI tools, your updated profile will be provided as context.
